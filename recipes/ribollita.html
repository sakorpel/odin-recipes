Ribollita

Description

A tasty vegetable soup from Toscana, Italy.

Ingredients

  • 300 g white beans
  • 2 red onions
  • 4 gloves garlic
  • 4 pieces of celery
  • 2 carrots
  • 300 g kale
  • olive oil
  • 1 tsp. fennel seed
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 400 g tomato sauce
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups dry bread pieces
  • small slices of parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Mince onions and garlic well. Fry them in olive oil. Cut carrots, kale, and celery into small slices.
  2. Add the vegetables to a pot with fennel seed, black pepper, and salt, and simmer them for 10 minutes.
  3. Add tomato sauce, rinsed beans, and broth. Simmer for half an hour.
  4. Add the pieces of bread and simmer for 15 minutes. Stir the soup once in a while, and add some water if needed.
  5. Pour some olive oil on each portion and serve.
