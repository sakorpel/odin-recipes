Coleslaw

Image: 'Coleslaw in a white Bowl Top View' by Marco Verch is licenced under CC BY 2.0

Description

A yummy salad, especially with hamburgers!

Ingredients

  • 1 cabbage
  • 2 carrots
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup creme fraiche
  • 2 tsp. apple vinegar
  • 1 T Dijon mustard
  • a pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Cut cabbage in half and rinse it well. Slice it into small pieces. Peel and grate carrots.
  2. Mix mayonnaise, creme fraiche, apple vinegar, mustard, and salt. The sauce should have a strong, sweet, and acidic taste.
  3. Put the vegetables in a large bowl and mix them well with the sauce.
  4. Put the salad in the fridge overnight, or at least for a couple of hours.
