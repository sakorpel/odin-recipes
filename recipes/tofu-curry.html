Tofu Curry

Description

Chop onions, garlic, bell pepper, and one chili. Grate ginger. Add them to a pan together with the spices. Fry them in oil for five minutes. Add tomato puree, coconut milk, and lime juice, and let the sauce cook for 15 minutes.

Fry tofu on a separate pan in oil and cook rice. When the sauce has cooked, season, add tofu and spread fresh cilantro on top. Serve with rice.

Ingredients

  • 400 g tofu
  • 370 g tomato puree
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 2 onions
  • 2 gloves garlic
  • red bell pepper
  • red chili pepper
  • lime
  • canola oil
  • 1 T ginger
  • 1 tsp. turmeric
  • 1 tsp. coriander seed
  • 1 tsp. mustard seed
  • 1 tsp. cumin
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • fresh cilantro
  • jasmin rice
