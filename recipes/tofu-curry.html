Tofu Curry

Description

This curry is fast and easy to make. You can enjoy it after a day at work or prepare it when you want to serve something delicious for friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 400 g tofu
  • 370 g tomato puree
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 2 onions
  • 2 gloves garlic
  • red bell pepper
  • red chili pepper
  • lime
  • canola oil
  • 1 T ginger
  • 1 tsp. turmeric
  • 1 tsp. coriander seed
  • 1 tsp. mustard seed
  • 1 tsp. cumin
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • fresh cilantro
  • jasmin rice

Steps

  1. Chop onions, garlic, bell pepper, and chili. Grate ginger.
  2. Fry the above ingredients in oil for five minutes together with spices.
  3. Add tomato puree, coconut milk, and lime juice, and let the sauce cook for 15 minutes.
  4. Fry tofu on a separate pan in oil and cook rice.
  5. Season, add tofu and spread fresh cilantro on top when the sauce has cooked. Serve with rice.
